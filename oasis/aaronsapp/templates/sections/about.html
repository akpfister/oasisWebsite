What is Oasis?

The Phi Kappa Tau fraternity of California State University, Chico holds a philanthropic concert every fall in order to raise money and donate to the Serious Fun. The concert is held in the backyard of their mansion on 611 W 5th St, Chico, CA from 9 p.m. to 12 a.m. Proceeds from the event go to Serious Fun, a non-profit organization that helps send terminally ill kids to camp.

Ticket sales always begin 2 weeks before the concert on Chico State campus and continue until the day of the event at the front door. We provide professional security that check for weapons and alcohol to help ensure the safety of everyone attending and working the event.

About Serious Fun

https://www.seriousfunnetwork.org/

Serious Fun is a non-profit organization whose main objective is to send terminally ill children and their families to summer camps all around the world, for free! America, Hungary, France, and the United Kingdom are just some of the countries that have camps that enrich the lives of not only the children affected, but also their family members as well. Any donation amount will help out tremendously in putting a smile on childrens' faces who need it.
